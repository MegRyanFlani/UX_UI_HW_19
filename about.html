About Me

Contact Me: megryanflani@gmail.com

Hi, I'm Meg Ryan Flanigan, & these are things about me!

My passion for all things creative and artistic began as early as I can remember, sketching Sailor Moon and building playdough..things. But I was convinced I was going to be a doctor. After a few months in a program for aspiring medical professionals, a desicion was made; the creative field was a better fit.

My time at SCAD was exciting, and I utilized many of their extracuriculars; I spent three months traveling and studying in France, attened several workshops presented by top professionals in many fields, traveled to Japan for a small, select group project, and presented in several local galleries.

After freelancing for many years (as well as bartending, which is still very useful for holiday parties), I began a deep dive into UX/UI, inspired by a long-time interest in how people think and act. Being able to apply a creative approach and great looking aesthetic to those thoughts and actions is something I enjoy doing. The goal is to continue doing those things, while learning how to to do them better.

My other interests include the occasional bartending gig, selling my art at festivals, running, hiking, and exploring places with my friends.

More often than not, I am accompanied by my dog, Quinn.
